Thanasis Papakonstantinou

ABOUT

O Thanasis Papakwnstantinou gennithike to 1959 stin Elassona kai megalwse ston Tyrnavo[1]. Spoydase Mixanologos Mixanikos sto Aristoteleio Panepistimio THessalonikis. Ston strato arxise na morfopoiei ta prwta tou tragoydia.I prwti symmetoxi sti diskografia itan me ta tragoydia Legewnarios kai Mayros Gatos ston disko Diairesi tou Vasili Papakwnstantinou (prokeitai gia apli synwnymia). Prin asxolithei epaggelmatika me ti mousiki ergazotan ws ergolavos dimosiwn ergwn. Zei sti Larisa, einai pantremenos kai exei didymous enilikous gious, enas ek twn opoiwn, o Kwnstantis, asxoleitai episis me tin mousiki kai exei grapsei kai o idios mousikes gia tainia megalou mikous. O Papakwnstantinou taxthike yper tou ''Oxi'' sto Elliniko dimopsifisma tou 2015.[2]

O Papakwnstantinou me ti Matoyla Zamani se synavlia sto Verolino (2012). O Papakwnstantinou exei synergastei me merikoys apo tous simantikoterous Ellines mousikoys. Stis ixografiseis, stis paragwges kai stis synavlies tou exoun symmetasxei metaksy allwn oi Giannis Aggelakas (fwni), Swkratis Malamas (fwni), Martha Frintzila (fwni), Giwrgos Mixail (fwni), Giannis Xaroylis (fwni), Mpampis Papadopoulos (kithares), Giwrgos (Mpantoyk) Apostolakis (kithares, pliktra, fysarmonika), Aleksis Apostolakis (tympana), Dimitris Mpaslam (kontrampasso), Dimitris Mystakidis (kithara, laoyto, tzouras), Fwtis Siwtas (fwni, violi, pliktra), Pantelis Stoikos (trompeta, kaval), Flwros Flwridis (pnefsta), Kwstas Parisis (kithares), Tasos Misyrlis (tselo), Mixalis Xaniwtis (mpouzoyki) , Florian Micuta (pliktra), Kwstas THeodwrou-Dine Doneff (kontra mpaso, krousta). Matoyla Zamani(fwni), Melina Kana (fwni).
